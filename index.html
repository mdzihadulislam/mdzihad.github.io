About Me

I am a dedicated and curious student passionate about innovation and solving practical problems. Working with electrical devices and systems gives me great satisfaction. I enjoy understanding how things work, taking systems apart, and creating new solutions to real-world challenges. Since childhood, I have participated in various science Olympiads like Physics, Math, and IT. Though I enjoy these intellectual challenges, my true passion is robotics, electronics, and technology innovation. I have completed several IT and IoT projects, always seeking ways to improve efficiency and quality of life. What drives me most is creating something new—whether designing smart sensor systems, automating processes with Arduino, or solving robotics challenges. I love soldering wires, coding circuits, and working late—not out of duty, but genuine interest. My goal is to become a multidisciplinary engineer who applies theory effectively to make a positive impact. I am committed to continuous learning and inspiring others to innovate with me.
